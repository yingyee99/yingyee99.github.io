Get to know us

B.Taughtion Centre has established in 1999 till present, it is the best education in the world and suitable for every student to learn and study. Our centre's goal is to help student enjoy the study when guiding them in a unique way without using the traditional way and we are proud to have over 700,000 students who agree!

Our Mission

At B.Taughtion Centre, we aim to give quality education and brilliant support of every one of our students, helping them in accomplishing their goals and understanding their dreams through quality education, the expertise of experienced tutors, a well-trained team of staff and a straightforward administrative system. We strongly accept that we are fit for controlling our students and giving them all holistic education.





How We Got Started

B.Taughtion Centre started up in 1999, and we've been on an epic ride ever since. From our beginnings with just three SPM subjects at Taman Maluri to lauching more SPM, and PMR subjects at Alam Damai in 2012, we've attempted to deliver a remarkable learning experience to our students. We owe a huge gratitude to our students and parents for going along with us on this wonderful journey, and we trust that you'll keep on being a piece of our story.
